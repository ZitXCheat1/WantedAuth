GDPR - How to delete WantedAuth Account?

While we're not beholden to the GDPR laws, we do believe privacy should be a fundamental human right.

You can delete your account at https://keyauth.cc/deleteAccount/. It does not require any intervention from us. Just enter your email (which we only stored hashed with SHA1😊) and you'll be emailed a link to confirm the deletion of your account.

As soon as you click the confirmation link, all of your data is wiped from our database. If you wish to come back, you should make a new account.

If your account was stolen and then deleted, you should contact support https://keyauth.cc/app/?page=support. We may be able to recover your account if it still exists in a recent database backup file.

We don't provide a way to fully export your data currently. This may come in the future. We do offer the ability to export certain application data to users who have purchased the Seller Plan https://keyauth.readme.io/reference/fetch-all-users
